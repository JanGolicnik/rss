what

blogsoni is a feed / link aggregator heavily inspired by lobste.rs where people submit links to blogs they like and they get polled every few hours for new stuff. users can also just submit cool sites by checking the "no rss" option when submitting.

its also an invite only system so you gotta get an invite from one of the members to be able to submit / comment.

why

human made content is really really awesome but its hard to keep track of.

thats why rss readers exist but i want to read blogs on the blog site itself, like the author intended it to be read.

at first i made this for myself but i dont see why it wouldnt be open to others, if you own a blog or know of any really cool ones please submit them !

extra

suggestions / bug reports are super welcome !

if your blog has been submitted here i want you to know that youre really cool and you get an invite by default. you can also always ask for it to be removed.

all users are public and the invite tree can be found here.
